# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** February 2026

## Data Collection

Litera Audiobook Player processes audio files stored on your device to provide audiobook playback functionality. The app may collect the following information:

- **Audio files**: Titles, artists, album names, and duration stored in metadata
- **Playback progress**: Listening position saved locally to resume playback
- **Favorites**: Books marked as favorites stored locally
- **User preferences**: Settings like theme, playback speed, and sleep timer preferences

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. The app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information to external servers except for optional cloud sync features.

## Optional Features

### Google Drive Sync
When enabled, this feature syncs your playback progress and bookmarks with your Google Drive account. This data is only shared with Google Drive and is not accessible to third parties.

### P2P Transfer
The optional P2P feature allows transferring audiobooks directly between devices on the same WiFi network. No data is transmitted to external servers.

## Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Contact

For privacy concerns, please contact the developer through the app's support channels.
